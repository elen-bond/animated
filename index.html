How It Works

How we underwrite a bond.

We issue. You use. They remember.

01 · Trigger

We find the moment

A funding round closes. An anniversary hits. A client renews. A new CTO is hired. We identify the trigger that makes your gift timely, not random. Good timing is half the bond.

02 · Underwrite

We structure the bond

A custom gift concept tied to your brand and your audience's world. Creative direction, packaging design, clever copy, branded inserts. Something they'll actually want to receive. Wall Street structures deals. We structure delightful surprises.

03 · Issue & deliver

We go to market

We source, produce, and assemble every gift. Then we reach out to your recipients, collect their delivery details through a personalized claim flow, and ship directly to their door. Tracked, timed, handled.

04 · Mature

The bond matures

They receive the gift. They laugh. They eat the pasta. They pour the drink. Then they do what every good bond obliges: they pay it back. With a thank you, a reply, a meeting. Your relationship just went up in value.
